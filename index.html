Tiktok

Tiktok is one of the most popular online social media sources to this day.

Tiktok provides millions of videos posted daily that is personalized to what everyone wants. On Tiktok, it provides a complex algorithm that is impossible to replicate since it tracks every interaction to the screen. For example, when you scroll to a new video, it tracks down to the milisecond on how long you are watching. It also sees if you like a video, it will eventually show something very similar and tracks how you interact to that as well so it will keep tracking stuff to see what you like to see so you see it frequently.

Tiktok is used by millions of people daily from this complex algorithm. People get everything they want just by how they interact to it. Searching, time spent on each video, liking, following, sharing, etc all help provide the system to what it wants to show again in the future. When you post a video, it it shown to a small amount of people at first to see the engagement with the video. If theres a certain percent of people who engage with it in that small amount of time, then it shows it to more people. People do love how this app works mainly because of this one big aspect that changes everything.
